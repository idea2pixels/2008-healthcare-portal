Are you at risk for heart disease and diabetes? What should you do?

Do you know whether your cholesterol and glucose values are healthy, too high, or too low? Knowing these values can help you identify serious risks and better manage health problems before they occur. Alere partners with BioIQ to provide you with the data you need to make healthy decisions.

How does it work?

Register with BioIQ
You will register at BioIQ’s website.
Receive at test kit
BioIQ wil mail you an at-home screening test for A1c and cholesterol risks. The test will arrive in a few days.
Use the screening test
You will need to extract a small blood sample. Detailed instructions will accompany the kit.
Send the kit to BioIQ
You send the sample back to BioIQ’s lab.
Access your results
You will receive notification when your results are ready. You can access them through your Alere profile.Your results will include your test results, lab report, and lorem ipus.
