The BioIQ A1c/Cholesterol Combination Test conducts two important tests from one blood sample. For heart disease, it measures the amount of total cholesterol, HDL, triglycerides, and the LDL cholesterol value. The test also includes an A1c/Diabetes test, which measures your average blood sugar over the past 90 days.

This is an important test to help you determine your risk for heart disease, especially for individuals diagnosed with, suspected of, or at risk of having any form of diabetes.
