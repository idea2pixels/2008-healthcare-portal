What is the Progress Tracker

The Progress Tracker is a one-stop resource for updating and tracking your behavior. If you use it frequently, you can gain valuable knowledge that can help you as you try to get healthier.

Why should I use it?

You can use the Progress Tracker to update information about your lifestyle habits such as your weight, eating habits, exercise routine or stress level. Keeping a daily journal can help you understand your habits. And it can help motivate you to improve.
