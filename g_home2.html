The BioIQ A1c/Cholesterol Combination Test conducts two important tests from one blood sample. For heart disease, it measures the amount of total cholesterol, HDL, triglycerides, and the LDL cholesterol value. The test also includes an A1c/Diabetes test, which measures your average blood sugar over the past 90 days.

This is an important test to help you determine your risk for heart disease, especially for individuals diagnosed with, suspected of, or at risk of having any form of diabetes.

The Chug-A-Jug Challenge is a month-long water drinking challenge that begins May 1 and ends on May 31. This program emphasizes the importance of water and proper hydration, especially during the hot summer months. During hot weather, it is important to increase your fluid intake ‚Äì regardless of activity level. What is the Progress Tracker?

The Progress Tracker is a one-stop resource where you can check your program status, find out which To-Do Lists you've completed and keep journals to track your progress.

Why should I use it?

You can use the Progress Tracker to keep updated information about your lifestyle habits such as your weight, eating habits, exercise routine and stress level. Keeping a regular journal can help you understand your habits and get you motivated to improve.
