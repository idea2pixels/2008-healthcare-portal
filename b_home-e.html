The Chug-A-Jug Challenge is a month-long water drinking challenge that begins May 1 and ends on May 31. This program emphasizes the importance of water and proper hydration, especially during the hot summer months. During hot weather, it is important to increase your fluid intake ‚Äì regardless of activity level. If you are exercising in a hot environment, be especially mindful and drink plenty of fluids to stay hydrated. Drinking water replaces the fluid our bodies need to work properly. Participants will enter the number of 8 ounces of water that they drink each day. The goal of this challenge is to earn 40 points during the 4 weeks.

40 points = 5 wellness credits
