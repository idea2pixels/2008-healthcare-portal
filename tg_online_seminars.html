View Seminar

Each month, a new online seminar is provided to help educate you on a variety of health and wellness topics. Each seminar takes approximately 10-15 minutes to complete. At the end of each section, you will be required to answer questions regarding the information presented. But don't worry - you can keep answering the questions until you get them correct. This is part of the learning process. You will need speakers or headphones on your computer to hear the audio component. New online seminars regarding health related topics are available each month. You will receive 1 Credit for each completed seminar, and you can earn up to 3 wellness credits total.
